Road Deaths in Victoria 2017-2019 a Visulaisation by Kevin Khuu

Road saftey has become increasingly more important as more and more people use Victorian roads each year. This visualisation aims to demonstrate the most dangerous situations, areas, age groups, gender and road users to help Vicctorian road users understand more about road saftey.

Most Dangerous Areas of Victoria

From the dotmap we can see that the most dangerous area for road users in Vicoria is Melbourne. As Melbourne is the captial city of Victoria it also has the largest population density and therefore the most traffic. The pletora of deaths within and near Melbourne is also due to the freeways leading into and going out of the city.

Most Dangerous Age Group and Gender

Drivers who are most at risk when on Victorian roads are those aged betweeen 40 and 64. Men are also much more likely to have a fatal car accident than women.
